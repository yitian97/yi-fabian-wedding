Our story

Our story started in 2020 during COVID, when there was nothing else to do. We became running partners, hitting the trails together to escape the monotony of lockdown. What started as a way to stay active and sane during those challenging times slowly transformed into something more. Mile by mile, run by run, we discovered a connection that went far beyond the paths we shared. Running became our love language, and we realized we were meant to be together. After two years, we adopted our baby Pipita, expanding our little family. Now, we're excited to take the next step and celebrate our love with all of you.
